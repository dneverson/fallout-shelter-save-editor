Fallout Shelter Save Editor

A free, open-source save editor for Fallout Shelter that runs entirely in your browser. Load your Vault1.sav file, edit your vault, and export it back to the game. Your save is decrypted, edited, and re-encrypted locally - it is never uploaded to a server.

What you can do

  • Edit dwellers: SPECIAL stats, level, happiness, health, equipped weapon, outfit and pet, appearance, and revive dead dwellers.
  • Edit your vault: caps, resources, food, water, power, Nuka-Cola, and lunchboxes.
  • Manage rooms: rearrange the layout, upgrade, apply themes, clear rocks, and repair.
  • Season passes: view every season's full reward track, including past seasons, and claim rewards you missed.
  • View your vault's family tree with relationship and genetics stats the game never shows you.
  • Browse full catalogs of every weapon, outfit, pet, junk item, and recipe in the game.
  • Repair corrupted saves with a built-in save health check.

Works with saves from PC, Android, iOS, and Switch. Free and open source (MIT). No accounts, no ads, no telemetry.
